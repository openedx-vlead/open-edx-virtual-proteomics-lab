After spotting on the MALDI plates , samples are allowed to dry for 30 min . Then the instrument is switched om and the MS analysis is performed according to the following steps.

  1. Click on " Launchpad" and then open "Kompact software".
  2. Go to instrument.
  3. Open " Aquisition Window".
  4. Click on "Exp. Tech".
  5. Click on "Open Door".


 

Figure 4. Representation of the software Window used for MALDI instrumentation for defining the experimental tech in Aquisition Mode.

    1. Insert the MALDI target plate (face up with the cut - off corner to the front ).
    2. In Exp. Tech , click close door.
    3. Allow it to create vaccum.
    4. In Exp. Tech windows : Linear or Reflectron , depending on sample (for less than 10,000 amu use reflectron mode).
    5. Set the mass range e.g. 10-5000Da.
    6. Then go to firing tab , set the power to 50 , profiles 1000 ns shots to 2.

    Figure 5: Representation of the software window used for MALDI instrumentation for defining the parameters of firing tab in Aquisition mode.

    1. Set the "Ion gate" OFF { for post -source decay (PSD) analysis ion gat should be kept on }.
    2. Then right click on the yellow target in the Aquisition window and " select go to location " and type the location of pep-mix , e.g. D1.
    3. Click on "processing" from main tab then "peak processing". Set typically smoothing off (for PSD it is average ) and select baseline subtraction.
    4. Click on "Monoisotopic peak" .Select "All display" and click "Apply to".
    5. Click on "Fire" . During the aquisition , Check the resolution of the highest mass standard peak.
    6. To check the resolution during an aquisition , click "suspend" to pause the aquisition. When the resolution has been measured , click " resume" to continue the aquisition . For peptide standard ACTH fragment 18-19 (m/z 2465.20), the rsolution should be > 5000 . If resolution is much lower then reduce the laser power to improve resolution.
    7. Move the target to find a " Sweet Spot ". Use clear data button to restart the accumulation once a satisfactory position has bee found on the sample spot.
    8. Once a satisfactory spectrum is obtained , click " Abort " to stop aquisition.
    9. Click " Store " to save the spectrum .
    10. In calibration window create a calibration file . Insert the mass of calibration standards , calibrate and save it.
    11. Now go to " Location " . Type the location for sample . And Click of " FIRE" and Look for a sweet spot for sample by moving the cursor . Once a satisfactory spectrum has been obtained , click " Abort and Store" to stop and save the spectrum.
    12. Result of the MALDI-TOF analysis can be saved either as ASCII files or the mass pick list can be saved manually. Further data - base analysis for identification of the protein has been disscussed in Exp. 7.
